Research interests

Personalized learning

I consider there is no grand purpose in our existence. Therefore, the only reasonable path I see for mankind is the one that achieves the maximum possible overall hapiness. Now, with hapiness I refer to the possiblity of setting goals in an autonomous way and having the freedom and the resources to go after these goals. In order to achieve this, Artificial Intelligence is a fundamental tool, since it is the only way to generate personalized learning systems that could allow an individual to idenfity and enhance goals, abilities, understanding of relevant knowledge and plans to fullfil those goals. The need for these personalized systems is evident, given that in order to achieve an overall happiness, each person should be taken into account, which is something that standard educational methods can't afford to do.

Artificial Intelligence

Given the nature of our existence, there seems to be 2 grand tasks we are compelled to fullfil: to model the world we perceive and to use this models in order to control our perceptions at will. What is not clear to me is what is the final purpose of this 2 tasks, given that tthe total understanding and the perfect control would rather make pointless our existence. Not without reason, Asimov's Multivac deems necessary to create the universe from the beginning.
